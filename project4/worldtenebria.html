Culture

The culture of Tenebria is mainly inspired by the cultures of the ancient Celts, the Germanic tribes, with Norse pagan to fill in the gaps left by the non-written-language users.

Interesting Segue

Tenebria is the home of a TON of Agronomers in addition to Stygians, and is home to Messis' shrine. This is believed to be because of the extreme lack of sunlight in the region that makes the services of an Agronomer nearly mandatory.
