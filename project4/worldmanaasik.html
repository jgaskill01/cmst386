Manaasik

Important Locations

  1. Nāśapātī
  2. Sthana
  3. Ṭā'iṭana
  4. Kalidasa
  5. Three Islands of Phala, Ghara, and Thapa

Culture

The culture of Manaasik is mainly inspired by the cultures of Southeast Asia, as well as from Rajput-era india.

Fun Fact

The ruling elite of Manaasik are known to exile political rivals for ridicuous reasons to the Addled Lands, where they are forced to wander for the rest of their lives as if they are impotent.
