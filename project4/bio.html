Author's Bio

Jake -- At a Glance

Who am I?

My name is Jake, and I'm an author, game designer, and general everyman. I have too many hobbies to count, and too much good fortune. My wife is my life, and my animals make that life better.

What are my goals?

I hope to bring joy to others through my creations. That's really it. Need there be more?

Why Fantasy?

Truth be told, there's plenty of interesting things in the real world, but I live there. No matter what I write if its based in the world I live in, its still reality, if contrived. The worlds in my head? Those are what could be, and they represent hope at their core.
